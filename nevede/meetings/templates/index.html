With Nevede, you can easily schedule an event.

No more tons of organizational emails among participants cluttering your inbox!

  2. Each participant goes to the event web page and expresses her/his availability.
  3. The web page gives a quick view on the best timeslot for the meeting.
  4. Bing! Everybody meets at the agreed timeslot.
